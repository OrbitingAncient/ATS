Basic Features

Making an Outgoing Call

  1. Lift the receiver or press Speaker.
  2. Press Float to access an outside line.
  3. Dial the number (e.g., 555-123-4567).

Receiving an Incoming Call

  1. When the phone rings, lift the receiver or press Speaker.

Accessing Voicemail

  1. Press Mail Box.
  2. Follow prompts (e.g., press 1 to play messages).

Using the Speakerphone

  1. Press Speaker for hands-free mode.
  2. Press Speaker again to end.

Identifying Your Phone Number

  1. Dial 800-444-4444.
  2. Listen to the automated announcement.

Identifying Your Extension

  1. Press Feature.
  2. Press 9 twice.
  3. Check the display for your extension and name.

Advanced Features

Call Park

  1. While on a call, press Park.
  2. Note the orbit number shown (e.g., 61-63).
  3. From another phone, press Park and dial the orbit number.

Call Forwarding

  1. Press Setup Menu.
  2. Select Call Forward.
  3. Choose a type:
    • All Calls: Sends every call to the new destination.
    • Busy/No Answer: Forwards if you’re on another call or don’t answer.
    • No Answer: Forwards only if unanswered after several rings.
    • External Number: Forwards to an outside number using codes 90-99.
  4. Enter the destination (e.g., extension 123 or code 90).
  5. Press Enter.

Speed Dial

Using Speed Dial:

  1. Lift the receiver or press Speaker.
  2. Press Speed Dial or dial a code (90-99).

Transferring a Call

  1. While on a call, press Transfer.
  2. Dial the extension (e.g., 123).
  3. Announce the call (optional), then hang up.

Do Not Disturb (DND)

  1. Press Hold/DND to enable (key lights up).
  2. Press again to disable.

Voicemail to Email

Request voicemails as email attachments by contacting IT at extension 204.

Troubleshooting

Phone Not Ringing

  • Check ringer volume: Press Volume Up while idle.
  • Ensure DND is off: Press Hold/DND if lit.
  • Verify call forwarding isn’t set to “All Calls.”

Unable to Access Voicemail

Press Mail Box and follow prompts. Contact IT at extension 204 if it fails.

Unused Features

Show Unused Features

Conference Calling

  1. While on a call, press Conference.
  2. Dial the next person’s number.
  3. After they answer, press Conference again.
  4. Repeat to add more people.

Call Pick-Up

  • Direct Extension: Press Call Pickup, enter the extension.
  • Internal Group: Press Call Pickup, select Internal Group.
  • External Group: Press Call Pickup, select External Group.

Note: Call Pickup button not available. Contact IT to add it.
